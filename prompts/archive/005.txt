  Recommended Implementation Sequence

  Phase 1 — Safe constants/cleanup (1–2 hours, zero behavior change):
  MV-1 through MV-5 (name all the magic values), P-5 (self-describing registry), D-3 (standardize to_thread), D-4, D-5, D-6 (extract small helpers), M-6 (remove duplicate log setter), M-9 (.get("phone", "")),
  M-10 (remove unused templates from app.py)

  Phase 2 — Moderate effort, low risk (half day):
  D-1, D-2 (shared helpers in plugins and session), E-1 through E-5 (improve exception handling), M-3 (key _pending_run by ID), M-4 (cache Google service), P-1 (Protocol typing + get_fields), C-4 (shared Jinja2
   instance), P-3 (merge check_wa_triggers)

  Phase 3 — Design changes (1–2 days, coordinate carefully):
  C-1 (decouple session from scheduler via callbacks), C-2+C-3 (remove DB access from plugins), P-2 (unify add_trigger), E-6 (step timeout), M-2 (timezone-aware datetimes throughout), M-1 (lazy DB engine init)
